Once the holiday season concludes, our team provides a timely and careful teardown service. We treat your property with the same respect as we did during installation, ensuring every light and wire is removed efficiently without leaving anything behind.

Our Teardown Process

  • Scheduled in Advance: We schedule your takedown appointment in early January, so you know exactly when to expect us.
  • Careful Removal: Every clip, cord, and bulb is gently removed to prevent any damage to your home, roof, or landscaping.
  • Organized Packing: We meticulously organize, label, and pack all components of your custom display for secure transport and storage.
  • Final Inspection: Before we leave, we conduct a final walkthrough to ensure your property is left in its original, pristine condition.
